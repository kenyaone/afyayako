❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Will I get a job after completing the course? ▼
Yes! MTTI CNA graduates have excellent job placement through our partnerships with major hospitals and clinics in Kenya (Moi Hospital, Nakuru Medical Center, private clinics). Our reputation in the healthcare industry means employers actively recruit our graduates.
Is the MTTI certificate accepted in hospitals? ▼
Absolutely! MTTI is TVETA-accredited (TVETA = Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority). This means our CNA certification is recognized and required by all Kenyan hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities. Your certificate is valid nationwide.
Can I work while studying? ▼
Yes! Our training is flexible. Classes are offered in evenings (6pm-8pm) and weekends (Saturday-Sunday). Many students work during the day and study at night. Some even start part-time healthcare jobs during their training.
What's the salary after graduation? ▼
Average starting salary is KES 40,000-50,000/month in government hospitals, KES 50,000-80,000/month in private clinics, plus tips. Your salary grows with experience. Many of our graduates earn KES 80,000+ per month after 2-3 years.
How do payment plans work? ▼
We offer 3 payment options: (1) Full KES 59,000 upfront, (2) KES 20,000 down + KES 13,000/month for 3 months, (3) KES 15,000 down + KES 8,000/month for 6 months. You can start immediately after the first payment.
