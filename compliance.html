🏥 Afya Yako Siri Yako

Ministry of Health Compliant Telemedicine Platform

Afya Yako Siri Yako is a fully MOH-compliant telemedicine platform providing anonymous mental health services, addiction recovery, and gambling counseling across Kenya. Our system implements comprehensive safeguards, professional oversight, and data protection mechanisms aligned with Ministry of Health standards.

✅ Professional Licensing & Verification

  • All therapists KMPDC Licensed (Kenya Medical Practitioners & Dentists Board)
  • CPB Verified (Counselors & Psychotherapists Board certification)
  • Real-time verification badges on professional profiles
  • Automatic license expiry tracking & renewal reminders
💡 Impact: Patients trust verified professionals; MOH standards for qualified practitioners met.

✅ Incident & Complaint Management

  • Patient adverse event reporting with severity levels (low → critical)
  • Professional complaint filing with investigation workflows
  • Admin investigation dashboard with status tracking
  • Disciplinary protocols: warnings, suspensions, terminations
  • Complete audit trail of all complaints & resolutions
💡 Impact: Ensures patient safety, MOH accountability met, full traceability.

✅ Data Protection & Privacy

  • AES-256 encryption for all Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • End-to-end encrypted sessions (E2E certified)
  • Session recording consent (explicit patient approval required)
  • No WhatsApp/SMS — all communication secure within platform
  • Configurable data retention (mood logs: 365 days, consultations: indefinitely)
  • Automatic deletion audit trail with reason & operator logs
💡 Impact: GDPR-equivalent standards, patient data security guaranteed.

✅ Professional Supervision (MOH Guideline 9)

  • Supervision dashboard tracking supervisors & supervisees
  • Supervision sessions logged with dates, duration, notes
  • Compliance alerts for unsupervised practitioners
  • Escalation protocols for clinical oversight
💡 Impact: Ensures clinical oversight & professional accountability.

✅ Response Time & Service Level Agreements

  • Default 2-hour response time SLA for consultations
  • Automated tracking of professional response times
  • Performance metrics visible to administrators
  • Escalation workflows if SLA breached
💡 Impact: Guarantees timely care & service consistency.

✅ Quality Assurance Framework

  • Daily quality metrics recording (client satisfaction, response times, completion rates)
  • Assessment quality scoring (0-100%)
  • Admin dashboard with KPI tracking
  • Data-driven quality improvement protocols
💡 Impact: Continuous improvement, measurable clinical outcomes.

✅ Parental Consent & Child Protection

  • Parental consent form required for minors
  • Age verification at signup
  • Parent notification options for sensitive cases
  • Child protection workflows aligned with MOH standards
💡 Impact: Protects minors, complies with child protection laws.

✅ Accessibility & Language Support

  • Kiswahili support (manual + Google Translate)
  • Mobile-first design — PWA (Progressive Web App) installable
  • No WhatsApp dependency — works on any browser
  • Low-bandwidth optimized for rural areas
💡 Impact: Reaches all Kenyans; aligns with MOH digital health equity.

✅ System Security & Compliance Documentation

  • HTTPS encrypted communication
  • Role-based access control (admin, professional, patient)
  • Audit logging of all system actions
  • Secure password policies for staff
  • Compliance dashboard for MOH audits
  • Full regulatory transparency & traceability
💡 Impact: Enterprise security, MOH audit-ready at all times.
