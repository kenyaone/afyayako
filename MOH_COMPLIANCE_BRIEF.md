# Afya Yako Siri Yako
## MOH Compliance & Regulatory Adherence Brief

---

## Executive Summary

Afya Yako Siri Yako is a **fully MOH-compliant telemedicine platform** providing anonymous mental health services, addiction recovery, and gambling counseling across Kenya. Our system implements comprehensive safeguards, professional oversight, and data protection mechanisms aligned with Ministry of Health standards.

---

## 1. Professional Licensing & Verification

✅ **All therapists are verified and licensed:**
- **KMPDC Licensed** — Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Board verification
- **CPB Verified** — Counselors and Psychotherapists Board certification
- **Real-time verification badges** displayed to patients on professional profiles
- **Automatic license expiry tracking** and renewal reminders

**Impact:** Patients trust verified professionals; MOH standards for qualified practitioners met.

---

## 2. Incident & Complaint Management

✅ **Comprehensive reporting system:**
- Patients can **report adverse events** with severity levels (low → critical)
- **Professional complaints** filed formally with investigation workflows
- **Admin investigation dashboard** with status tracking (open → investigating → resolved → closed)
- **Disciplinary action protocols** — warnings, suspensions, terminations with documented dates
- **Complete audit trail** of all complaints and resolutions

**Impact:** Ensures patient safety, MOH accountability requirements satisfied, full traceability.

---

## 3. Data Protection & Privacy

✅ **Military-grade encryption:**
- **AES-256 encryption** for all Protected Health Information (PHI)
- **End-to-end encrypted** sessions (shown in sidebar)
- **Session recording consents** — explicit patient consent required before any recording
- **No WhatsApp, SMS, or insecure channels** — all communication through secure platform

✅ **Data retention policies:**
- **Configurable retention periods** per data type
- Mood logs: 365 days
- Assessments: 365 days  
- Consultations: Indefinitely (anonymized after 2 years)
- Automatic deletion with audit trail logging

**Impact:** GDPR-equivalent standards, patient data security guaranteed.

---

## 4. Professional Supervision

✅ **MOH Guideline 9 — Mandatory Supervision:**
- **Supervision dashboard** tracks supervisors and supervisees
- **Supervision sessions logged** with dates, duration, and notes
- **Compliance alerts** if professionals lack supervision assignment
- **Escalation protocols** for unsupervised practitioners

**Impact:** Ensures clinical oversight and professional accountability.

---

## 5. Response Time & Service Level Agreements (SLA)

✅ **Quality monitoring:**
- **Default 2-hour response time SLA** for professional consultations
- **Automated tracking** of consultation response times
- **Performance metrics** visible to administrators
- **Escalation workflows** if SLA breached

**Impact:** Guarantees timely care and service consistency.

---

## 6. Quality Assurance Framework

✅ **Daily quality metrics recording:**
- **Client satisfaction scores** (0-100%)
- **Response time metrics** (vs SLA baseline)
- **Session completion rates**
- **Assessment quality scores**
- **Admin dashboard** displays daily KPIs

**Impact:** Continuous improvement, data-driven quality management.

---

## 7. Parental Consent for Minors

✅ **Safeguarding under 18s:**
- **Parental consent form** required before minor registration
- **Age verification** at signup
- **Parent notification option** for sensitive cases
- **Child protection workflows** aligned with MOH standards

**Impact:** Protects minors, complies with child protection laws.

---

## 8. Accessibility & Language Support

✅ **Inclusive platform:**
- **Kiswahili support** (manual + Google Translate) for broader reach
- **Mobile-first design** — PWA (Progressive Web App) installable
- **No WhatsApp dependency** — works on any browser
- **Low-bandwidth optimized** for rural areas

**Impact:** Reaches all Kenyans; aligns with MOH's digital health equity goals.

---

## 9. System Security

✅ **Enterprise-grade infrastructure:**
- **HTTPS encrypted** communication
- **Role-based access control** (admin, professional, patient)
- **Audit logging** of all system actions
- **Secure password policies** for staff accounts
- **Test account management** via secure admin panel (no hardcoded credentials)

**Impact:** Prevents unauthorized access, meets MOH cybersecurity expectations.

---

## 10. Compliance Documentation

✅ **Full traceability:**
- **Compliance dashboard** for admins with incident KPIs
- **Data deletion audit trail** showing what was deleted, why, when, by whom
- **Professional discipline records** with documented sanctions
- **Session recording consent registry**

**Impact:** MOH can audit the platform at any time; full regulatory transparency.
